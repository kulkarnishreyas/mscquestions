1️⃣ Advanced Applications of FDA

Core Application Areas

💼 Investment Mgmt

ML-driven portfolio optimisation, real-time adjustments

⚠️ Risk Mgmt

Predictive analytics for credit, operational, market risks

🛡️ Compliance & Fraud

NLP/AI flagging anomalies in real-time

🎯 Customer Personalisation

Tailored loans, credit cards, investments

Effectiveness

  • Improved decisions + risk mitigation
  • Operational efficiency & regulatory support
  • Better forecasting & portfolio optimisation
  • Real-time fraud detection
  • Automated KYC (Know Your Customer): FDA tools streamline customer-identity verification — document OCR, biometric matching, sanction-list screening — making onboarding faster while satisfying regulatory mandates
  • Regulatory reporting automation: Bots and pipelines compile periodic disclosures (capital adequacy, transaction logs) directly from source systems, cutting reporting cycles from weeks to hours

2️⃣ Blockchain in Financial Technology

Core Principles

🌐 Decentralisation

No central authority — distributed nodes verify

👁️ Transparency

All participants see the ledger

🔒 Immutability

Cryptographic hashes prevent tampering

🤝 Consensus

Network agreement (PoW, PoS, PoC, etc.)

Impact on Financial Services

  • Disintermediation — direct peer-to-peer, faster cross-border
  • Enhanced Security — cryptographic, real-time fraud detection
  • Transparency & Audit — clear transaction history
  • Innovation — smart contracts, tokenisation, fractional ownership

Challenges

  • Regulatory uncertainty across jurisdictions
  • Legacy system integration
  • Scalability for high-volume transactions
  • Privacy conflicts with GDPR-like laws
  • Shortage of blockchain+finance expertise

4️⃣ Integrating ML with Financial Analytics

ML Integration Process

  1. Data Collection & Preparation — ethics, consent, privacy
  2. Feature Engineering — credit scores, transaction patterns, derived ratios
  3. Model Selection — regression/classification/neural networks
  4. Training & Validation — historical + unseen data
  5. Deployment — into live financial systems
  6. Monitoring & Maintenance — ongoing updates

Challenges

📊 Data Quality

Fragmented, multi-source — cleaning takes time

⚖️ Compliance

GDPR + opaque models = risk

🔍 Transparency

Use SHAP/LIME

👥 Talent Gap

Combined finance + ML expertise rare

⚙️ Integration

Legacy IT systems hinder adoption

Success Framework

  1. Data Management — cloud, real-time, secure
  2. Model Development & Validation — SHAP/LIME, rigorous testing
  3. Ethics & Compliance — embed early, audit regularly
  4. Continuous Monitoring — ModelOps platforms

📝 Summary & Exam Tips

🎯 Key Takeaways

  • Advanced FDA spans: investment mgmt, risk, fraud, compliance, personalisation
  • Blockchain principles: decentralisation, transparency, immutability, consensus
  • Use cases: SBI lending, RBI CBDC, Mahindra supply chain, YES Bank Corda
  • AI trends: ML predictive, NLP, robo-advisors, AI+blockchain integration

  • Static workforce skills can't keep pace with rapid tech change
  • Privacy-by-design must be built in, not bolted on later
